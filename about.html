Rich Pang

Rich has over a decade of experience working with complex, naturalistic datasets in neuroscience. During his PhD at the University of Washington he analyzed and modeled insect flight and dopamine dynamics. During his postdoc at Princeton University he developed methods for leveraging natural behavior data to discover neural algorithms, as well as mathematical theories for the neural basis of memory. Website: rkp.science.
