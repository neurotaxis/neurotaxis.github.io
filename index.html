About the founder

Rich Pang has over 10 years of experience analyzing complex data in neuroscience. During his PhD at the University of Washington he analyzed and modeled insect flight trajectories tracking odor plumes as well as multi-dimensiona, time-varying dopamine signals in fruit flies. During his postdoc at Princeton University he developed methods for leveraging natural behavior time-series to discover novel bioplausible neural algorithms.
