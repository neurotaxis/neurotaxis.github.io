Frequently asked questions

Do you provide custom modeling/data analyses?
Yes! If you're interested in a service not listed on our services page, just write us to inquire.

Do we get to see the code used for analyzing our data?
Yes! We return all code and processed data to you. We strive to write reliable, interpretable code that can be easily reused and modified by others.

How does authorship work?
By default we do not expect authorship on papers published by your research group. However, if a natural collaboration should arise as a result of our contributions we would be happy to discuss the possibility of co-authorship.

Is this going to take away projects from students and postdocs?
Not at all. Our goal is to help give scientists at all stages more time and space to think deeply about their most important research questions. We achieve this by providing the opportunity to contract out certain tasks in the lab so that you and your group have more time to be creative and push the frontier. We do believe that it's fundamental to understand the complexities and nuances of data analysis and modeling and highly encourage early-career scientists to implement these methods on their own (check out our blog or online courses!). Time is a limited resource, however, and in a lab there can be many more tasks than people to do them. Luckily, we're here to help.

How much do your services cost?
We bill either daily or per project. Please write us to inquire about specific rates. We also offer a sliding scale if funding is an issue.

Can I pay for your services using my grants/startup?
Yes! The easiest way to pay is through the university purchase order system. After inquiring about a service we will prepare an official quote that can be attached a PO with the revelant chartstring or funding source indicated. Please note that in order for our services to be billed against a PO, Neurotaxis, LLC must be established as a vendor at your university. This process can take a month or two (but it is only required once), so please let us know early if you are interested in using our services.
